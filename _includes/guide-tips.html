• Always check your Rice Status and talk to Tauemon.
  • Use Anti-Toxicity (Spring/Renowed Water, Aqueous Droplet) Fertilizer to counter Salt Damage.
  • Use Presicide (Eradicating Remedy, Salt + Water, Fish Mint) Fertilizer to counter some Disease (Rice blight, Rice stripe virus).
  • Use Immunity (Revitalizing Remedy, Salt + Water, Aqueous Droplet) Fertilizer to counter some Disease (Rice Ragged Stunt Virus, Pecky Rice Disease).
  • Use Herbicide (Reducing Remedy, Aqueous Droplet, Rice Bran + Fish Mint) Fertilizer to counter Weeds and Herbs.
  • Weather: Pray for sunny weather to counter Spindly.
  • Weather: Pray for cool weather to counter Heat damage or open both gates.
  • Use Fertilizer with Rotten Food to boost Stats, Salt (to balanced out Presicide) and Spring/Renowed Water (to balanced out Toxicity) (beware of Salt damage).
  • Use Ingredients in your Fertilizer that expires and don't give negative effect like Acorns, Grasshopper or Bee Larva.
  • Try to use more Natural Presicides like Fish Mint, Aqueous Droplet or Garlic to avoid Salt damage.
  • Use can farm Moonlit Stone at night early in the game, dropped by rabbit deamons and Aqueous Droplet later in the game, dropped by water enemys.
