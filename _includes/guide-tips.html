• Always check your Rice Status and talk to Tauemon for tips.
  • Use less Salt in your Fertilizer to avoid Salt damage or skip using Fertilizer for this day.
    While Salty Food gives you better boost, its saline can make the poop/base-fertilizer salty :)
  • Use Eradicating Remedy to cure Rice blight and Rice stripe virus.
  • Use Revitalizing Remedy to cure Rice Ragged Stunt Virus, Pecky Rice Disease and Spindly.
  • Use Reducing Remedy to cure Overgrown and Spindly or avoid having too much soil nutrients (full triangle).
  • Weather: Pray for sunny weather to counter Spindly.
  • Weather: Pray for cool weather to counter Heat damage or open both gates.
  • Use Fertilizer with A LOT Rotten Food to boost Stats, Salt and Spring/Renowed Water. (beware of Salt damage).
    (Try to use Aqueous Droplet instead of Salt and Spring/Renowed Water)
  • Try to use more Natural Pesticides like Fish Mint, Garlic ot Ginger.
  • You can put Ingredients into your Fertilizer that expires and don't give negative effect like Acorns, Grasshopper or Bee Larva.
  • Use can farm Moonlit Stone at night early in the game, dropped by demons rabbits and Aqueous Droplet later in the game, dropped by water enemies.
  • When checking Ride Status, Dehydration is a GOOD thing, see Farming Guide for more details.
